Tailored Terahertz Solutions From Broadband Coverage to Narrowband Power(broadband terahertz and narrowband terahertz sources)!

in the rapidly evolving field of terahertz technology, one size never fits all. Whether your application demands narrowband high-power sources or full-waveguide-bandwidth coverage, we deliver complete, customized solutions tailored precisely to your system requirements.

When your application demands concentrated energy at specific frequencies, our narrowband high-power sources deliver exceptional performance. Typical configurations include W-band (92–96 GHz) at 10 W, 220 GHz at 400 mW, and 340 GHz at 100 mW. These sources are ideal for long-range radar, high-data-rate communication, and active imaging systems where signal-to-noise ratio is critical. Custom power levels and frequency points are available upon request.

For applications requiring wide spectral coverage, our broadband terahertz sources are engineered across multiple waveguide bands. Standard options include 75–110 GHz (500 mW), 110–170 GHz, 170–260 GHz, and 260–400 GHz. For even broader flexibility, we also offer 60–90 GHz, 90–140 GHz, 140–220 GHz, 220–325 GHz, 325–500 GHz, and 500–750 GHz (0.1 mW). Each of these bands can be customized to your exact center frequencies and output power levels, ensuring optimal matching with your existing system architecture.

A source alone is not enough. For every frequency band we support, we provide matching mixer-based receiver links to form complete terahertz transceiver chains. Whether you are building a compact radar front-end, a terahertz communication link, or a high-resolution imaging system, our integrated solutions guarantee seamless source-to-receiver compatibility. Each link can be tailored—from waveguide interface choices to local oscillator integration—to fit your specific system-level needs.

We understand that every terahertz system has unique constraints: size, weight, power consumption, or spectral purity. That is why customization is at the heart of everything we offer. From a single broadband module to a fully integrated narrowband transceiver, we work with you to design, test, and deliver the optimal solution. Contact us to discuss your frequency, power, and receiver requirements—your custom terahertz solution is just a conversation away.
